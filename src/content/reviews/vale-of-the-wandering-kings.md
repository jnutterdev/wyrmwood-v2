*Vale of the Wandering Kings* is not the kind of book that announces itself. It arrives quietly, settles in, and then doesn't leave.

This is literary fantasy in the best sense — a book that uses the genre's tools (a dying empire, a contested throne, magic that is older than anyone alive) to ask questions about continuity and loss that feel urgently contemporary.
