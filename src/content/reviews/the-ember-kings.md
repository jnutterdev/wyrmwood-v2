Every generation of fantasy readers waits for the book that makes them feel like they did reading fantasy for the first time. *The Ember Kings* is that book for 2026.

What separates it from the wave of secondary-world epics that have arrived in its wake is discipline. Author Sera Vance does not flinch from consequence. Every power has a cost that is paid in full.

The magic system — a form of heat-borrowing that leaves its practitioners progressively cold — is the finest in recent memory precisely because it is never explained. It is simply there, ominous and beautiful.
