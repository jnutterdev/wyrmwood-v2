The collective behind *Ash & Oracle* has never given interviews. They do not tour. They release records on cassette and vinyl only, through a label whose contact information is a PO box in a city that may or may not exist.

The music matches the mythology. This is folk metal that has metabolised its influences entirely — the fiddle lines, the choral passages, the occasional blast beat — and emerged as something that belongs entirely to itself.
